Our History

THE JOURNEY SINCE 1980

In Rasullapur, Tetultala Club was established in 1980,
and from that very year Durga Puja became its heart.
What started as a small gathering soon turned into the
grandest celebration of the village. The sound of the dhak,
the glow of lights, and the fragrance of dhuno filled the
air with devotion. Children waited eagerly for these
festive days, while elders shared memories of past pujas.
Women decorated the pandal with flowers, and men worked
together to keep the tradition alive. Cultural programs,
songs, and dances made every night vibrant and joyful.
Visitors from nearby villages came to witness the beauty and devotion.
The pandal became not just a stage for worship, but a place of unity
and pride. Year after year, the festival grew stronger, passing from
one generation to another. Today, Tetultala Club’s Durga Puja
still shines as the soul of Rasullapur.
